Short Biography

I am a PhD candidate at Electrical and Computer Engineering department in UMassD started from Fall 2022 (Supervisor: Prof. Honggang Wang, IEEE Fellow). Before joining at UMassD, I have been working at School of Computer Science and Engineering in Nanyang Technological University (NTU) as a full-time Research Associate. My research interests mainly include advanced communications, security, and edge intelligence techniques for Internet of Things (IoT) and connected vehicles. Besides, I am serving as a reviewer for several major IEEE journals, magazines, and conferences. I received my MS in Computer Science and BS in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Jahangirnagar University and International Islamic University Chittagong, Bangladesh, respectively. I am a graduate student member of IEEE, IEEE ComSoc, and IEEE VTS.
