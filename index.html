Short Biography

I am a PhD student at School of Computer Science and Engineering in Nanyang Technological University (NTU). Here, I have been affiliated with the Computer Networks and Communications Lab (CNCL) and working under Prof. Dusit Niyato and Prof. Jun Zhao. In my PhD research, I am involved in developing game theoretic approaches for the intelligent cyber-physical systems. My research interests are mainly focused on advanced communication, security, and resource allocation techniques for future cyber-physical systems and wireless networks. I am passionate about learning, teaching, and research. Besides, I am also serving as a reviewer for several major IEEE journals, magazines, and conferences. I received my MS in Computer Science and BS in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Jahangirnagar University and International Islamic University Chittagong, Bangladesh, respectively. I am a member of IEEE, IEEE ComSoc, and IEEE VTS.
